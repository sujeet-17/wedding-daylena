Weddings
what working with me is like...
From the time you reach out about your wedding you receive a pre wedding consultation with me where you can ask all the questions you have about your day and your photography. Let's get to know each other.

After you hire me, I will spend a lot of time getting to know your wedding day inside and out, asking your preferences and helping you figuring out timing for photos and lighting on your day.

I will be by your side capturing your memories all day, in the most artistic and documentary way. When the party is over and the guests are gone, you will be getting sneak peeks from me to keep re-living your day.

Your photos will be delivered in a few weeks and you can share them with friends and family while your emotions are still strong.

My hope is to continue our friendship long after your wedding day and document the rest of your special moments to come.
ALL PACKAGES INCLUDE...
Wedding Day Coverage, Pre-wedding consultation, full printing rights, image ownership, heirloom usb and a full resolution online gallery delivered within three weeks of your big day.
